The **Willow Creek Cafe** is a location in [[Courage & Resolve]] and [[Dreams of Fame]].

## Profile
Located on the second floor of an upscale building in residential [[Willow Creek]], the Willow Creek Cafe is a relaxing spot locals will meet at to discuss things over lunch or coffee.

[[Amelia Brown]] regularly met [[Jo Kauffman]] here.

## Trivia
- The Willow Creek Cafe lacks an exterior, and is actually located on the second floor of the [[Presley House]] in the [[Courage & Resolve]] save. The second floor was torn out for the sake of this cafe because no member of the Brown family would ever have a reason to set foot up there.
