The **BFFs House** is a location in [[Dreams of Fame]]. It's the home [[Liberty Lee]], [[Travis Scott]], and [[Summer Holiday]] moved into after high school.

## Profile
Right after graduating high school, [[Liberty Lee]], [[Travis Scott]], and [[Summer Holiday]] all decided to move in together, each having their own personal reasons for doing so. They approached a [[Adrienne Brooks|local realtor]], and she sold them this house, with [[Charlie Scott|one of Travis's moms]] footing the brunt of the bill.

After choosing to move in with [[Zoe Presley]] in her [[Presley House|home]], the house went on the market again. It likely went through a series of owners, but it eventually ended up vacant, and was eventually demolished. The [[O'Riley Community Garden]] later opened in the place it had once stood.
