**Giovanna's Husband's Grave** is a location in [[Dreams of Fame]]. It's the supposed burial place of [[Giovanna Cardwell]]'s [[Granny's Husband|husband]].

## Profile
A plot of land located somewhere in [[Willow Creek]], this is where [[Giovanna Cardwell]] claims she buried her [[Granny's Husband|husband]]. [[Zoe Patel]] followed Giovanna here and confronted her after she and her husband broke into her home and slept in her bed.

Giovanna refuses to reveal where exactly this location is, and Patel was unwilling for different reasons, leading to the final resting spot of Giovanna's husband being lost. This is a significant reason in why many doubt the man ever existed.
