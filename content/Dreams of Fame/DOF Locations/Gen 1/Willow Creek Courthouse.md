The **Willow Creek Courthouse** is a location in [[Dreams of Fame]]. It's the courthouse of [[Willow Creek]], presided over by the [[Judge]].

## Profile
The Willow Creek Courthouse played host to Case: Zoe vs. Zoe, the battle of rights to *A Day in the Life of Granny*, with [[Zoe Patel]] suing [[Zoe Presley]]. Zoe Presley ended up winning the case due to a timely entrance made by [[Giovanna Cardwell]].
