The [[Britechester]] branch of **Beauchamp Law** is a location in the first chapter of [[Lies and Deceit]]. 

## Profile
[[Felix Beauchamp]] opened a new branch of [[Beauchamp Law]] in [[Britechester]] as his law firm continued to see increased success. 

Due to [[Judith-Noelle Gruber]]'s high performance, [[Genevieve Beauchamp]] flew in to personally promote her to regional manager of the Britechester branch.
